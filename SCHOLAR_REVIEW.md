# Scholar & Community Review Guide (GDD §12.6) — pre-launch requirement

The process for the review that must happen before public launch. Timing:
after the vertical slice looks real (art landed), before soft launch —
budget 4–8 weeks of calendar time for it.

## 1. Who to approach ⚑ MANUAL
- Ideal panel: **two reviewers** — one scholar/student-of-knowledge with
  tariqa affiliation (the game draws on Naqshbandi-lineage teachings; a
  reviewer from or familiar with the Muhammadan Way community is the
  natural fit given nurmuhammad.com is the recorded lore source), and one
  community advisor who games (catches tone issues scholars won't).
- This is **paid work** — budget line ~$500–1,500 total. Respect their time
  with a prepared packet (below), not "please play our game."
- Warm paths: the community behind the lore source; local teachers you
  trust; MAS/ISNA-adjacent creatives; Muslim game-dev communities.

## 2. The review packet (I generate this when you say go)
A single document + the web build link containing:
1. **The project's intent** — dawah by attraction; GDD §1.1 language policy
   and the strict lore caveat (fantasy, inspired-by, links to source).
2. **Every piece of religious-adjacent content, extracted**: all 25 Codex
   entries verbatim with their source links; the twelve season names + the
   teachings they were drawn from; all Vice true-names; order names/kits;
   Guardian dragons framing; the Twelve Moons calendar mechanics (tabular
   Hijri decision — needs their blessing); Ramadan-season design (lightened
   Deeds, no purchase pressure).
3. **The ethics record**: gambling-free ruling + §12.9 no-paid-randomness;
   deterministic combat rationale (§4.4); the full §12 charter.
4. **Specific questions** (so the review produces decisions, not vibes):
   - Is any Codex entry's framing objectionable despite the caveat?
   - The in-game LINKS to nurmuhammad.com — does the community welcome
     this? (Ask them directly; it's their platform.)
   - Season 3 "The Kingdom" / season 8 "The Beloved" — comfortable?
   - Tabular calendar vs sighting for season boundaries — acceptable?
   - Vice true-names as boss titles — any line crossed?
   - The word "Trance" for aḥwāl-inspired ultimates — better word?
   - Anything in the art direction brief that needs a rule we missed?
5. **How to respond**: a simple approve / revise / remove per item.

## 3. Process ⚑ MANUAL
1. Send packet + web build link; offer a call after they've read.
2. Every "revise/remove" becomes a PROGRESS.md item; I implement; changed
   items go back for a second look (changes only — respect their time).
3. Record the outcome in GDD §12.6 (who reviewed, when, what changed) —
   this record is also your answer to any future community question about
   diligence.
4. Ask whether they wish to be credited or anonymous. Honor it.

## 4. What NOT to do
- Don't launch marketing that leans on the dawah identity before this
  review — the identity must be earned first.
- Don't treat approval as permanent: the §6 expansion protocol includes
  re-review for new lore-heavy content (Part 2 especially — GDD §6.2
  already flags it).
